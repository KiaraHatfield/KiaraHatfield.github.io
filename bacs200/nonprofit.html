What is our value proposition?

We aim to feed citizens with fairness

What is our vision?

Feeding America supports local food banks with food, funds, and partnerships to help them reach more of our neighbors. For more than 40 years, we have responded to the needs of individuals struggling with food insecurity in this country. In times of uncertainty, we have not wavered from our mission to end the fight against hunger.
